Cycle the Continental Divide!

Come join bike enthusiasts from around the world to celebrate the 20th anniversary of the USA Continental Divide! It's the opportunity of a lifetime to see the wilds of the country and experience its geography in a way like no other.

Details

The Great Divide is a nearly 3,000 mile route spanning from Banff, Canada, to Antelope Wells, New Mexico. It's rigorous! It's remote! And it will be the most breath-taking weeks of your life. The trip is broken up into 7, week-long sections, each week concluding with a day off the bikes for rest, relaxation, or tourism.

The tour group will be supported by two vans containing food, equipment, and yes, a mechanic. The vans will be available the majority of the trip, excepting the portions of the trail that deny vehicle access. During those portions, we will make sure you're loaded up with everything you need, and you will have guides at your disposal in case of emergency.

Pricing

The total cost of the full, 7-week tour is $9,600. Cost includes all food and emergency equipment, as well as 17 nights at motels.
